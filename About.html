LEARN ABOUT HAVEN BEES

We are a social and sustainable beekeeping and honey production enterprise. We offer training to local beekeepers and non-beekeepers, we purchase their honey and process it for retail and wholesale. Our mission is to grow as a company in high-quality bee products, services, and improve the lives of more than 10,000 beekeepers while protecting and improving the vast wilderness and forest areas of Tanzania.
